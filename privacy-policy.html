Privacy Policy

CPGX, LLC d/b/a CPG Fast Track  ·  Effective Date: January 1, 2026

CPGX, LLC, doing business as CPG Fast Track ("CPG Fast Track," "we," "us," or "our"), respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you visit our website, join our community, or otherwise interact with our services (collectively, the "Services").

This Privacy Policy is a stand-alone document but should be read together with our Terms of Service, which govern your use of the Services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, information collected automatically, and information from third parties.

Information You Provide to Us

You may provide personal information when you:

  • Visit our website
  • Join the CPG Fast Track membership
  • Create or update a community profile
  • Subscribe to our email newsletter
  • Participate in surveys, forms, polls, or community discussions
  • Contact us for support

This information may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Payment-related information (processed by Stripe; we do not store full payment card details)
  • Community profile information
  • Messages, posts, and other content shared in the community
  • Responses to surveys or forms

Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Services, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages viewed and actions taken
  • Referral URLs
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from third-party services used to operate our business, including:

  • Circle (community hosting and engagement)
  • ConvertKit (email communications)
  • Stripe (payment processing)
  • Analytics and advertising partners (e.g., Meta)

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not intentionally collect:

  • Sensitive personal data (such as Social Security numbers or government IDs)
  • Health or medical information
  • Precise geolocation data
  • Personal information from children under the age of 18

Our Services are intended for adults only.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Services
  • Manage memberships and accounts
  • Process payments and billing
  • Send transactional communications (e.g., account updates, service notices)
  • Send marketing and promotional communications (with opt-out options)
  • Facilitate community participation and engagement
  • Conduct analytics and understand how the Services are used
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and community rules
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Marketing Communications

Members and subscribers may receive:

  • Transactional emails, such as billing notices, account updates, or important service communications
  • Marketing emails, such as newsletters, announcements, and promotions

All marketing emails include an unsubscribe link. Opting out of marketing communications does not affect your receipt of transactional emails.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Analyze site traffic and usage
  • Understand user behavior
  • Support marketing and advertising efforts

We do not currently use a formal cookie-consent banner. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though disabling cookies may affect certain features of the Services.

6. How We Share Information

We may share personal information:

  • With trusted service providers who help us operate the Services (e.g., Circle, Kit, Stripe, analytics tools)
  • With advertising platforms for marketing and remarketing purposes
  • To comply with legal obligations, court orders, or lawful requests
  • In connection with a merger, sale, or transfer of assets

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

7. Community Content & Visibility

Information you share within the CPG Fast Track community (such as posts, comments, or messages) may be visible to other members, depending on the settings and context.

You are responsible for the information you choose to share publicly or semi-publicly within the community.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide the Services
  • Fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy
  • Comply with legal, accounting, or reporting obligations

CPG Fast Track is based in the United States and primarily serves U.S.-based users, but international users may access the Services.

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you

Requests may be submitted to hello@cpgfasttrack.com. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

California residents may have additional rights under applicable state privacy laws, including the right to request information about data collection and sharing practices.

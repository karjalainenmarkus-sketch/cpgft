Who It's For

Here's who gets the most out of CPG FastTrack.

Early-Stage Founders

You've launched your product and have some traction, but you're figuring out distribution, marketing, and operations largely on your own. You need a room where people actually get what that's like.

Scaling Operators

You're past $1M and facing a new set of problems — retail expansion, team building, co-packing, capital. The decisions are bigger and the stakes are higher. You need people who've been here before.

Multi-Brand Operators

You've built one brand and you're thinking about what's next — a second line, a new channel, an exit. CPG FastTrack gives you the strategic sounding board and network to make the right moves.
